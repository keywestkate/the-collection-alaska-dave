Homer, Alaska — The End of the Road

The Guide Team
at the Horizon.

Homer sits at the edge of the road system — and the edge of something vast. The Kachemak Bay, the open Pacific, Katmai, and the Gulf of Alaska spread before you. Our captains and guides bring decades of deep-water and coastal experience to every trip.

The Collection Founder

David Lisi

Dave Lisi built The Last Frontier Collection from the ground up — starting with the Kenai River and expanding outward along the Kenai Peninsula until he reached the edge of the continent. Homer represents the culmination of that journey: where the road ends and the sea begins.

A licensed guide and lodge operator since the early 2000s, Dave has fished every major run on the peninsula — from trophy rainbow trout on the upper Kenai to Pacific halibut in Kachemak Bay. His reputation for putting clients on fish and delivering a premium experience is what built this collection one property at a time.

At Homer, Dave partners with the captains and operators who know these waters best — because the deep ocean demands specialists. Every Flat Fish Lodge charter, every Baycrest Lodge stay, every Driftwood inn booking connects back to the same standard: world-class Alaska, guided properly.

Why Homer

The End of the Road Is the Beginning of Everything

Pacific Ocean

Homer sits on Kachemak Bay with direct access to the Gulf of Alaska. Halibut over 200 lbs, lingcod, rockfish, and Kachemak Bay silver salmon are all within reach of the harbor.

World-Class Views

Baycrest Lodge and Ocean Bluff offer some of the most dramatic vistas in Alaska — panoramic bay views, volcanic peaks, and the constantly changing light of coastal Alaska. This is where guests come to breathe.

Flyout Country

Homer is an air-service hub. Katmai bear viewing, remote river fly fishing, and wilderness lodges across the Alaska Peninsula are all accessible via float plane or bush plane from Homer airport.

Certifications & Standards

Licensed. Insured. Accountable.

US Coast Guard Licensed
All charter captains hold current USCG Masters licenses for commercial passenger vessels operating in Kachemak Bay and Gulf of Alaska waters.
ADF&G Licensed
Alaska Department of Fish & Game licensed sport fishing guide and charter operator. All operations fully permitted for Kachemak Bay and Kenai Peninsula fisheries.
First Aid / CPR Certified
All guides and captains are current in first aid and CPR. Remote and offshore operations require highest safety preparedness — we take that seriously.
Commercially Insured
Full commercial liability coverage for all charter and guide operations. All lodge properties carry appropriate hospitality insurance.
Drug Screened
All captains and guides operating under The Last Frontier Collection are drug screened. Non-negotiable when guests are offshore or in wilderness environments.
1% for Planet
One percent of revenue is donated to environmental nonprofits dedicated to protecting Alaska's wild fisheries and coastal ecosystems. The fish come first.
