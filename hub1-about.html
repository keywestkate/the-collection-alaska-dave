The Guide

David Lisi

Dave Lisi is a year-round Cooper Landing resident and one of Alaska's most recognized fishing guides. A licensed Kenai River guide, published fly fishing industry contributor, and children's author, Dave has dedicated his career to sharing Alaska's fisheries with guests from around the world.

He is an AFFTA (American Fly Fishing Trade Association) member and was recognized by Fishmasters as a top Alaska guide in 2020. The operation he built — Cooper Landing Fishing Guide, LLC — has become the foundation of what is now The Last Frontier Collection.

Dave guides on the Upper, Middle, and Lower Kenai River and the Kasilof River, targeting every species Alaska's waters produce — from trophy rainbow trout to world-class King salmon. His philosophy is simple: the client's experience comes first, every time.

Standards

Every Guide. Every Trip.

Kenai River Guide School Certified
All guides complete the Alaska Board of Fisheries certified guide school requirement.
US Coast Guard Certified
Federal certification for all guides operating motorized vessels on Alaska's navigable waters.
First Aid & CPR Trained
All guides maintain current wilderness first aid and CPR certification for remote river environments.
Drug Screened
Pre-employment and random drug screenings for all guides — no exceptions.
Commercially Insured
Full commercial liability coverage on all guide operations, vessels, and equipment.
1% for the Planet Member
1% of gross annual sales donated to environmental protection organizations. The fishery that feeds us, we protect.
