Activities

Beyond the River

The Soldotna corridor sits at the center of the Kenai Peninsula. Wildlife, wilderness, and world-class recreation in every direction.

Wildlife
Kenai Wildlife Refuge

1.97 million acres of protected wilderness. Moose, bears, eagles, and Dall sheep within minutes of Hub 2. Guided wildlife viewing available through the concierge.

Off-Road
ATV Trails

Miles of mapped ATV and UTV trails through boreal forest and river flats. Equipment rentals coordinated through the Hub 2 concierge team.

Water
River Excursions

Float the Lower Kenai by drift boat — non-fishing scenic trips available. Kasilof River floats, and Skilak Lake day trips departing from Hub 2.

Hiking
Kenai Peninsula Trails

Dozens of trails ranging from easy riverside walks to backcountry routes into the Kenai Mountains. Custom guided hike packages available.

Aerial
Floatplane Tours

Glacier flyovers, remote lake drop-offs, and fly-in fishing charters. Coordinated through the circuit concierge for Hub 2 guests.

Corporate
Group Retreats

Hub 2 is the preferred node for corporate fishing retreats and team-building events. The Moose & Spruce property accommodates 30+ guests exclusively.

Recovery & Wellness
