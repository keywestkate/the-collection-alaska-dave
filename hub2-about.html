The Guide

Same Guide. New River.

Dave Lisi's guide operation extends from the Upper Kenai at Cooper Landing all the way down to the Kasilof River corridor in Soldotna. The Kasilof Salmon Guide service is the Hub 2 extension of Cooper Landing Fishing Guide, LLC — same team, same commitment, different water.

The Kasilof River is one of Alaska's most prolific King salmon streams — with a distinct run separate from the Kenai, offering access to trophy Chinook on a wilder, less crowded river. Dave and his team also work the Lower Kenai River from the Soldotna corridor, targeting sockeye, silver, and steelhead.

All guides operating out of Hub 2 hold the same certifications — Kenai River Guide School, US Coast Guard, First Aid/CPR, drug screened, and commercially insured. A 5-star rated operation with zero exceptions on standards.
